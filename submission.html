All Categories, All Genres, All Languages, are Accepted from All Continents. English Subtitle is Compulsory.

We promote new filmmakers, students, professionals by exchanging of information, cultures & ideas between National & International filmmakers through film submissions. For more details on films, Short films, feature films, documenatries, web series & music videos submissions please visit our international film festival link (http://www.mwfiff.com/submission) , You can also submit your short films, feature films, etc directly through our ONLINE FORM at the biggest international film festival in Mumbai India.

Films,Short films, feature films, documenatries, animation films, kids films, web series & music videos, Music Videos, etc can also be submitted directly in our international film festival through FilmFreeway, Festhome, WFCN OR (youtube , Vimeo, Google Drive, like platforms) and sending us link (and password if protected),entry form & press kit in the biggest international film festival in India - MWFIFF

In case of using submission platforms like FilmFreeway, Festhome, BELOW PRESS KIT items MUST be E-mailed to - contact@mwfiff.com, in order to complete the submission. DCP / J2K File would be Compulsory if the film is selected for Screening in the Film Festival or Extra Cost would be charged if the filmmakers wants us to convert the file for the same. Extra Cost For the Film Conversion Would be $25 per movie. All Non-English Films must have English Sub-titles for Festival Presentation.
