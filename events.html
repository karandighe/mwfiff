Events

This event will be held for 2 days with LIVE Screening in the heart of Mumbai City, Maharashtra, India. We will be screening movies selected by our Jury on the day of the Screening and on the final day of the event i.e. the Award Ceremony With Entertainment Programme, we will be presenting Awards and Certificates to the Nominated & Winners selected by our Jury. Selected Nominated Participants will be receiving ONLY Certificates and Winners will be receiving Awards & Certificates. All Awards and Certificates will be given by our Respected Panel of Jury listed on our website, Respected Chief Guest, VIP's and Bollywood Celebrities Present on the day of the Event.

About Us

MWFIFF intends to organize a festival that connects the film fans & film makers with the entire world, especially the serious filmmakers commercial or non commercal & their associates so as to bring about greater understanding of the Art of film making.
