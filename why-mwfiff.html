Why MWFIFF

There is only one phrase that answers the question. We are different.

How, you may ask? MWFIFF has completely different set of rules when it comes to recognising talented actors, actresses, directors, screenplay writers and other technicians. This is possible because we have on board a jury which is diversified, grounded to the roots and immensely talented in their own areas of expertise. We have well known bollywood personalities in our Jury Panel such as Anup Jalota, Jaspinder Narula, Gufi Paintal, Rahat Jafri, Pt. Suvashit Raj and Yogesh Lakhani as some of the honourable members.

This list will obviously change every year and with it we shall introduce some of the compelling film personalities who share our passion and vision for providing a unique platform to talented individuals.

The most important USP of Moonwhite Films International Film Fest is this International Film Festival is a NON - COMMERCIAL Film Festival which is being organised since 2018 in Mumbai, India. We do not sell any tickets for entry for screening at Movie Theatre or at the auditorium for Award Ceremony along with the entertainment programme.

Moreover, Moonwhite Films International Film Fest is based on the idea of providing outstanding and unbiased platform to film fraternities from across the globe. Our vision is to bring countries together through creative exchange and ideas which includes individuals, companies, film magazines and journalists and a plethora of entities who work behind the scenes. We also aim to award creative folks based on merit and their achievements and not simply because of their past laurels.

Moonwhite Films International Film Fest is the beginning of that change. In the years to come the world will see more talented folks belonging to the exciting world of cinema, through our dedicated prism, thorough our kaleidoscope and through the lens of our honourable jury members.

So, come and be a part of this change, and if you have it in you, then you belong on the MWFIFF podium.

We are waiting for you!

About Us

MWFIFF intends to organize a festival that connects the film fans & film makers with the entire world, especially the serious filmmakers commercial or non commercal & their associates so as to bring about greater understanding of the Art of film making.
