Our Team

Devashish Sargam – Director MWFIFF

Devashish Sargam who is Born and Brought up in Mumbai, Maharashtra is multi-faceted personality with distinct creative attributes. He belongs to family who are synonymous with music and musicians. Interestingly, Bhushan Kumar of T-Series launched his Debut Music Video Album which was Super Hit specially the Song "KHAT' which crossed billions hit on youtube through mesmerizing songs and music in India and across the globe which fetched him immense popularity and accolades. Soon after this huge success Devashish Sargam another Album "Aye Sorry Tujhe Salam" was a Massive Hit and he was consecutively Artist Of The Month for 2 months on B4U Music TV Channel. Devashish Sargam has also worked with Radio Mirchi 98.3 FM for three years as Concept Writer and team member for their super hit show called 'Sun Taro Ki Dhun' with non other then RJ Rahat Ji and RJ Suvashit Raj.

Incidentally, Devashish Sargam is also a Award Winning Film Writer, Composer, Actor and Award Winning Film Director.

In 2015-16, 'Kill Hand' his debut short film was nominated in as many as seven countries, and finally won the "Award of Recognition" in Canada. In the subsequent year he was again nominated and been awarded for another short film titled 'The Stone Girl', and as such story was there in BBC London News.

His streak of laurels continued. Last year, he added another feather to his talented cap when he was chosen the Best Writer & Director for his creative shorts titled 'The Masked Prostitute' and also been awarded for the Best Director and Best Awareness Short Film " Dengue The Killing Machine" 2017

Being in the thick of the action most of the time Devashish Sargam is well versed with the nitty-gritty of film making such as script writing, direction, editing and of course the experience of struggling days always keep him rooted to the ground. It is because of these varied attributes which made it possible for him to conceive and create a one of a kind film festival which aims at being unbiased and creative-centric.

And the journey has just begun!!!

About Us

MWFIFF intends to organize a festival that connects the film fans & film makers with the entire world, especially the serious filmmakers commercial or non commercal & their associates so as to bring about greater understanding of the Art of film making.
