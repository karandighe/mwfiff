*Submission Guidelines

Films/Music Video in any languages other than English, must have English Subtitles, otherwise they shall not be considered.DCP / J2K File would be Compulsory if the film is selected for Screening in the Film Festival or Extra Cost would be charged if the filmmakers wants us to convert the file for the same. Extra Cost For the Film Conversion Would be $25 per movie.

The Festival accepts only films/music videos without watermarks.

Only Trailers or promos cannot be submitted for selection process.

Films/Music Videos which have no online presence can be submitted by sending two full HD DVDs by courier to the Festival office along with a hard copy of this form duly filled. Kindly label the DVDs with title, director name, duration, phone number and email id on it.

For submissions coming from outside India, the packages must be marked, “For Festival & Cultural Purpose only, No Commercial value”. selection.
